Chicken Tempura

My family loves all types of fried chicken — especially the boneless varieties. Easy to eat and endlessly delicious, chicken tempura is a fun twist on the typical chicken tenders you may be used to. Serve with rice, ponzu sauce, and some thinly sliced cabbage during dinnertime, and no one will able to resist. Tempura batter can be made entirely with all-purpose flour, but swapping in a bit of potato or corn starch makes for an especially light crust. If you don’t have it, all-purpose flour works just fine.
